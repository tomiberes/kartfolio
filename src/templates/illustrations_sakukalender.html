SAKU CALENDAR

Client | Saku Brewery
AGENCY | The Division
CREATIVE DIRECTOR | Tõnu Sikk

Saku Brewery is one of the main beer producers in Estonia. They comissioned a calendar for sending to their clients and collaborators. The identity of the beer is based on stressing the history and traditions that goes into making the beer and from there the idea is derived from. The concept of the calendar was to imitate historically important artists works using their main beer glass. My assignment was to make the illustrations for it.

The best part of the project was doing research on the techniques of the various artists and using the knowledge to reproduce that. The artist that were used were: Cezanne, Gaugin, Tolouse-Lautrec, Bernard, Marc, van Gogh, Degas, Kirchner, Monet, Seurat, Dürer and Mane.
