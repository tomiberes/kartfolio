Tegude kalender is a student startup project. They make calendars, where every day is different positive action. Every day has assigned to it, what a person should do on that day, for example: "Call your mom". The logo is derived from the concept, combining actions and ideas. As the calendar has illustrations from local artsts the style and feel of the logo itself is more illustrative:
