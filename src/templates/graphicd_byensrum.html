Byens Rum Logo

Client | Byens Rum

This is a winnng work for a logo competition for Byens Rum. They are an organization that provides creative people working spaces. The inspiration came from layout schemes for apartments.

As it was an extremely creative flexible organization the idea was to reflect that in the design. Thus the design became more flexible and something that could be used online in a way that would be different from a standardized logo. Based on that an animated gif was made for web use. In addition a proposal was made for how the logo could be used.
