Dawnwave identity

Dawnwave is a radio show born out of love of alternative music. They plays everything between punk-rock, noise, no-wave and yes-wave. The show is hosted by two people and airs in Thursdya mornings 7.30. From there the logo is derived with the name to create contrast.

The imagery accompanying the identity should be disturbing, dark and noisy. Dawnwave is not your traditional explosion of clever colours and contrast, somethng that should come past in the resulting design.

Dawnwave should be available anytime anywhere. So it needed also an app design. Like in the images, the contrast has been turned down to make it noisy and dark just like the show.
